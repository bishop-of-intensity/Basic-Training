Training Parameter: Tempo

The time it takes to execute that part of the movement

Or The Speed you try to do the reps in

‚ùó Note: If you don't feel that the targeted muscle/muscles
Are not working hard enough, use longer tempos,
ex 303, 505, until you "get" the movement

We Track 3 Moments:

  1. The Contraction / 'Positives'
  2. The Pause Between Reps
    For Pushing/Extension Movements: The Bottom of the rep
    For Pulling/Flexing Movements: The Top of the rep
  3. The Lowering / 'Negatives'

You can note them as 101, 513, etc,
Each of the numbers being one of the 3 moments

Examples

  • 101 = Banging reps as fast as possible
  • 111 = [Usually for Pushing Movements]
    Explosive Reps /w Pause (at the bottom for pressing)
  • 103 = Explosive positive, Controlled Negative
  • 313 = [Usually for Flexing Movements] Slow Heavy Contraction

30s-90s
